About us

The Piston SIG encompasses the non-circuit branches. It has two main groups:

Robotics and Automation Society

This society beckons enthusiasts in the fields of robotics and automation. Inculcated only a year ago into IEEE NITK, RAS is a perfect platform for multidisciplinary work as both Robotics and Automation cannot be categorized under one specific SIG. Moreover,a sum of upto $2000 per annum will be provided directly by IEEE for projects under it.

MACH

This group undertakes projects in various fields such as Aerospace Engineering, Bio-Mechanical Applications, Heat and Energy Engineering,Automobile Engineering, Robotics,Structures and Materials, Chemical and Thermal Engineering,Control Systems and Renewable Energy.
